Hey! I'm Jonathan

I’m a software engineer with an enthusiasm for React, Redux, Rails and PostgreSQL. On a more personal note, I’m passionate about sailing. I usually race weekly in Long Beach with the Seal Beach Yacht Club. I’ve always been drawn to open water and have found being on the ocean with no engine noise very therapeutic. Working the bow has proven to be a great workout and very thrilling. Whether it’s programming or sailing, I enjoy being challenged and acquiring new skills. I believe problem solving and attention to detail are key qualities that have allowed me to excel in my passions. I love learning new technology and building projects that have a positive impact on my community. As a full stack software developer, I have enjoyed using Ruby, JavaScript, React/Redux, Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, CSS and HTML in my projects. I’m planning on learning Python next and strengthening my skill set in Express and Node.js.
